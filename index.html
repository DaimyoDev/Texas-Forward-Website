Become A County Chair

County chairs are responsible for representing the entire county for the Forward Party. No qualifications required besides passion and care for our movement.

Your job includes outreach for the party within your county, recruiting precinct chairs and establishing committees for your county. Activism for the party and promoting the Forward Party platform and mission. Provide reports to the State Executive Committee on how you will grow your county.

Become A Precinct Chair

Precinct chairs are responsible for representing a precinct for the Forward Party. You will have to contact the County chair if you have one. No qualifications required besides passion and care for our movement.

Precinct chairs must organize their precinct recruiting others to be a part of the precinct. You will participate within county wide meetings, hold recruitment events, activism, and outreach. Support local and county level candidates running for office.
